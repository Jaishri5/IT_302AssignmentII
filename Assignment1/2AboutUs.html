Mission

At Novel Xp, our mission is to provide a comprehensive and diverse collection of novels, spanning various genres to foster a vibrant reading community. We strive to create a user-friendly platform where readers can explore, discover, and immerse themselves in captivating stories from around the world.

Vision

"We aspire to create a global literary ecosystem where readers from all walks of life can seamlessly connect with books that resonate with their unique identities, interests, and aspirations, fostering a deeper appreciation for the power of storytelling."

History

NOVEL XP started in February 2024 with an IWT assignment to create a static HTML page. The website owner had an idea to connect readers with their passion for reading and provide collection of novels on a single website.

The website was completed a week before the deadline.

Its safe to say that the website creator has been succesful in acheiving both the goals ie; completing the assignment as well as igniting the passion for reading.
